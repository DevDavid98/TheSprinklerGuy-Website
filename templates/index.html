The Sprinkler Guy

Sprinkler Installation, Repair &

Landscaping

The Sprinkler Guy is a local small business that is family owned and operated. We thrive on meeting all your irrigation and landscaping needs. Anything from commercial to residential. We do small repairs to large installation to custom landscape design. With over 15 years of experience we take pride in our work to make your yard look great!

Main Services

We provide sprinkler repairs & installation as well as lawn maintenance, landscaping and custom design. We also provide outdoor stone work that include flowerbeds, patios, driveways and sidewalks. We trim trees and offer flower bed design and installation.
